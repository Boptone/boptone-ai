# Privacy Policy Additions - February 21, 2026

## Document Purpose

This document contains ironclad legal additions to the Boptone Privacy Policy to address new data collection and processing activities resulting from features implemented on February 21, 2026:
1. Shippo shipping integration (shipping addresses, tracking data)
2. SEO/GEO system (structured data generation, search engine indexing)
3. Agent API (third-party AI agent access to user data)

These additions must be inserted into the existing Privacy Policy document to ensure compliance with GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other data protection laws.

---

## Section 2.1 Information You Provide Directly - NEW SUBSECTION

### Shipping and Delivery Information

When you purchase physical products through BopShop or ship products as a seller, we collect the following shipping and delivery information:

**Shipping Addresses:** We collect complete shipping addresses for both buyers and sellers, including recipient name, street address, apartment/unit number, city, state/province, postal code, country, and phone number. Shipping addresses are collected at checkout for buyers and during product listing creation for sellers.

**Return Addresses:** Sellers must provide return addresses for products that may be returned by buyers. Return addresses include the same fields as shipping addresses.

**Address Validation Data:** We use address validation services to verify the accuracy and deliverability of shipping addresses. Address validation may result in the collection of standardized address formats, geocoding data (latitude/longitude coordinates), address type classifications (residential vs. commercial), and delivery point validation codes.

**Delivery Preferences:** We collect delivery preferences such as signature requirements, delivery instructions (e.g., "leave at door," "ring doorbell"), safe place instructions, and preferred delivery time windows.

**Package Information:** We collect information about packages being shipped, including package weight, dimensions (length, width, height), declared value, insurance coverage amount, and special handling requirements (fragile, perishable, hazardous materials declarations).

**Customs Information (International Shipments):** For international shipments, we collect customs declaration information including product descriptions, harmonized tariff codes, country of origin, declared customs value, and export/import documentation.

## Section 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically - NEW SUBSECTION

### Shipping and Tracking Data

When you use our shipping services, we automatically collect the following information:

**Tracking Information:** We collect tracking numbers, shipment status updates, carrier scan events, estimated delivery dates, actual delivery dates and times, delivery confirmation signatures (if applicable), delivery location coordinates, and proof of delivery photographs (if provided by the carrier).

**Carrier Selection Data:** We collect information about which shipping carriers and service levels you select, including carrier name (USPS, FedEx, UPS, DHL), service level (Ground, Priority, Express), shipping cost, estimated delivery time, and reasons for carrier selection.

**Shipping Label Data:** We collect information about shipping labels generated through our Service, including label creation timestamps, label format (PDF, PNG, ZPL), label purchase amount, postage payment method, and label void/cancellation events.

**Delivery Attempt Data:** We collect information about delivery attempts, including number of delivery attempts, failed delivery reasons, delivery exceptions (weather delays, address issues, recipient unavailable), and package rerouting or return-to-sender events.

**Shipping Performance Metrics:** We collect aggregated shipping performance data, including average delivery times, on-time delivery rates, delivery failure rates, customer satisfaction ratings for shipping, and carrier performance comparisons.

## Section 3: How We Use Your Information - NEW SUBSECTION

### Shipping and Fulfillment

We use shipping and delivery information for the following purposes:

**Order Fulfillment:** We use shipping addresses, package information, and delivery preferences to facilitate the shipment and delivery of physical products purchased through BopShop. This includes calculating shipping rates, generating shipping labels, arranging carrier pickup, and providing tracking information.

**Delivery Optimization:** We use shipping data to optimize delivery routes, select the most cost-effective and reliable carriers, predict delivery times, and reduce delivery failures. We analyze shipping performance metrics to identify and address delivery issues.

**Address Validation:** We use address validation services to verify the accuracy of shipping addresses, reduce delivery failures, and prevent fraud. Address validation helps ensure that packages are delivered to the correct locations.

**Customs Compliance:** For international shipments, we use customs information to generate customs declarations, calculate duties and taxes, and comply with export/import regulations. We may share customs information with customs authorities as required by law.

**Customer Support:** We use shipping and tracking data to respond to customer inquiries about order status, delivery issues, lost or damaged packages, and returns. Shipping data helps us investigate and resolve shipping-related disputes.

**Fraud Prevention:** We use shipping address data, delivery confirmation data, and shipping patterns to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions, including stolen credit card purchases, reshipping schemes, and address manipulation fraud.

**Analytics and Reporting:** We analyze shipping data to generate reports on shipping costs, delivery performance, carrier reliability, and customer satisfaction. These insights help us improve our shipping services and negotiate better rates with carriers.

## Section 5: Data Retention - NEW SUBSECTION

### Shipping Data Retention

We retain shipping and delivery information for the following periods:

**Active Orders:** Shipping addresses, tracking information, and delivery data for active orders (orders that have not yet been delivered or completed) are retained until the order is completed or canceled.

**Completed Orders:** After an order is completed, we retain shipping and delivery data for seven (7) years to comply with tax reporting requirements, facilitate returns and refunds, respond to customer inquiries, and defend against legal claims.

**Shipping Labels:** Shipping label data (label images, tracking numbers, postage amounts) is retained for seven (7) years for accounting, tax, and audit purposes.

**Address Validation Data:** Address validation results are retained for ninety (90) days to improve address validation accuracy and reduce redundant validation requests. After ninety days, address validation data is deleted or anonymized.

**Tracking Data:** Tracking event data (carrier scans, delivery confirmations, delivery exceptions) is retained for three (3) years to facilitate customer support, analyze shipping performance, and resolve disputes.

**Failed Delivery Data:** Data related to failed deliveries, lost packages, or damaged packages is retained for seven (7) years to support insurance claims, carrier disputes, and legal proceedings.

## Section 5: Data Retention - NEW SUBSECTION

### Structured Data and SEO Data Retention

We retain structured data and SEO-related information for the following periods:

**Structured Data (JSON-LD, Meta Tags):** Structured data generated from your content is retained for as long as your content remains publicly accessible on our Service. When you delete content or set it to "private," the associated structured data is removed from our sitemaps and search engine indexing within thirty (30) days.

**Sitemaps:** Sitemaps are dynamically generated and reflect the current state of publicly accessible content. Historical sitemap data is not retained.

**Search Engine Crawl Logs:** Logs of search engine crawler activity (Googlebot, Bingbot) are retained for ninety (90) days for security monitoring and performance analysis. After ninety days, crawl logs are deleted or anonymized.

**SEO Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized SEO analytics data (search rankings, click-through rates, keyword performance) is retained indefinitely for business intelligence and service improvement purposes. This data does not contain personally identifiable information.

**LLM Citation Data:** Data related to LLM citations of our content (if provided by LLM platforms) is retained for one (1) year for analytics and service improvement purposes.
