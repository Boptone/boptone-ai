## Slide 2: The Music Industry is Broken

**Artists are quitting because the math doesn't work**

- 1 million streams = ~$3,500 (before splits)
- Labels take 80-85% of master recordings
- Publishers take 50%+ of publishing rights
- Artists have zero access to their own fan data
- No direct relationship with fans

**Result**: 90% of artists earn less than $10,000/year from music

*"I'm ready to quit being a musician"* - Sentiment echoed across artist communities

## Slide 6: Boptone Audio Protocol (BAP)

**Direct Artist-to-Fan Streaming with Fair Revenue**

**How BAP Works**:
1. Artist uploads track (3-click upload with AI metadata extraction)
2. Audio stored on S3 with HLS streaming
3. Fans stream directly from Boptone
