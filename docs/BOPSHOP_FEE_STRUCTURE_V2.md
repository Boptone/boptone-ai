- **Boptone makes money from subscriptions** ($0-$149/month)

1. **Predictable revenue** - Subscriptions are more stable than transaction fees
2. **Artist-aligned** - We want you to sell more (doesn't cut into our revenue)
3. **Competitive** - Beats Bandcamp/Etsy on volume, comparable to Shopify
4. **Ecosystem play** - BopShop + streaming + distribution = sticky platform
