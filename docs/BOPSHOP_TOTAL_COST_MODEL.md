## üí∞ Cost Model: Physical Inventory vs. Print-on-Demand

### **Scenario A: Artist Pre-Orders Physical Inventory**
**Example: Artist bulk-orders 100 t-shirts at $5 each, sells for $25**

| Cost Component | Amount | Who Pays |
|----------------|--------|----------|
| **Bulk t-shirt cost** | $5.00 | Artist (upfront) |

**Profit margin:** $18.97 - $5.00 (COGS) = **$13.97 profit per shirt (55.9% margin)**

## üö¢ Shippo Integration Details

### **How Shippo Works**
- **Real-time shipping rates** from USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL
- **Discounted rates** (up to 90% off retail)
- **Label printing** + tracking automation
- **Address validation** to reduce failed deliveries

### **Typical Shippo Rates (U.S. Domestic)**

| Package Type | Weight | USPS | UPS Ground | FedEx Ground |
|--------------|--------|------|------------|--------------|
| T-shirt (envelope) | 4 oz | $3.50 | N/A | N/A |
| T-shirt (small box) | 8 oz | $4.50 | $7.50 | $8.00 |
| Hoodie (medium box) | 1 lb | $6.50 | $9.00 | $9.50 |
| Vinyl (flat mailer) | 12 oz | $5.00 | $8.00 | $8.50 |
| Poster (tube) | 8 oz | $5.50 | $8.50 | $9.00 |

### **Who Pays Shipping?**

**Option 1: Customer Pays (Recommended)**
- Shipping cost added at checkout
- Artist keeps full margin
- Standard e-commerce practice

**Option 2: Artist Pays (Free Shipping)**
- Artist absorbs shipping cost
- Reduces profit margin by $3.50-$9.00 per order
- Marketing strategy ("Free shipping over $50")

**Option 3: Hybrid (Flat Rate)**
- Artist charges $5 flat shipping
- Artist profits if actual cost < $5
- Artist loses if actual cost > $5

## üí∞ Revenue Model for Boptone

### **Boptone Makes Money From:**
1. **Subscriptions** ($0-$149/month per artist)
2. **Streaming protocol fees** (5% of per-stream revenue)
3. **DSP distribution fees** (10% of Spotify/Apple Music royalties)

### **Boptone Does NOT Make Money From:**
- ‚ùå BopShop platform fees (0%)
- ‚ùå Credit card processing markup (pass-through)
- ‚ùå Shipping markup (pass-through Shippo rates)
- ‚ùå POD markup (pass-through Printful/Printify costs)

**Why this works:**
- ‚úÖ **Artist-aligned:** We want you to sell more (doesn't cut into our revenue)
- ‚úÖ **Transparent:** No hidden fees, no markups
- ‚úÖ **Competitive:** Beats Etsy (12.5% platform fee), matches Shopify (0% platform fee)
- ‚úÖ **Ecosystem play:** BopShop + streaming + distribution = sticky platform
