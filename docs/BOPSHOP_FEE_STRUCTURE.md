# BopShop Fee Structure for Physical Goods
**Artist-First E-Commerce for Merch, Vinyl, and Physical Products**

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Core Philosophy

BopShop follows Boptone's artist-first model: **transparent pricing, no hidden fees, and artists keep the maximum revenue possible.** Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms that take 10-30% cuts, BopShop only charges:

1. **Credit card processing fees** (passed through to artist, not marked up)
2. **Small platform fee** (3-5% depending on volume)

## ðŸ’° BopShop Fee Structure

### **Option 1: Boptone Payments (Recommended)**
**Boptone handles all payment processing through Stripe Connect**

| Product Price | Credit Card Fee | Platform Fee | Artist Nets |
|---------------|-----------------|--------------|-------------|
| $25 t-shirt | $0.73 + $0.30 = $1.03 (2.9% + $0.30) | $0.75 (3%) | **$23.22 (92.9%)** |
| $50 hoodie | $1.45 + $0.30 = $1.75 (2.9% + $0.30) | $1.50 (3%) | **$46.75 (93.5%)** |
| $30 vinyl | $0.87 + $0.30 = $1.17 (2.9% + $0.30) | $0.90 (3%) | **$27.93 (93.1%)** |

**Credit Card Processing Fees (U.S.):**
- **Online orders:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **In-person (POS):** 2.6% + $0.10 per transaction

**Platform Fee:**
- **3% of gross sale** (covers hosting, bandwidth, order management, analytics)

**Total Artist Take:** **~93% of gross sale**

## ðŸ”§ Implementation Details

### **Payment Flow (Boptone Payments)**
1. Fan purchases $25 t-shirt
2. Stripe charges $1.03 processing fee (2.9% + $0.30)
3. Boptone takes $0.75 platform fee (3%)
4. Artist receives $23.22 instantly (93%)

### **Payment Flow (External Processor)**
1. Fan purchases $25 t-shirt
2. Artist's Stripe account charges $1.03 (2.9% + $0.30)
3. Boptone takes $1.25 platform fee (5%)
4. Artist receives $22.72 (91%)

| Hoodies | $40-$60 | $37.20-$55.80 (93%) | $36.40-$54.60 (91%) |
| Vinyl | $25-$35 | $23.22-$32.56 (93%) | $22.72-$31.85 (91%) |
| Posters | $15-$25 | $13.93-$23.22 (93%) | $13.65-$22.72 (91%) |
| Stickers | $3-$5 | $2.70-$4.62 (90-92%) | $2.64-$4.52 (88-90%) |

**Note:** Lower-priced items (<$10) have slightly lower artist take due to fixed $0.30 processing fee.
