# Boptone - Own Your Tone

**Mission Control for Your Artist Career**

## Purpose

Boptone empowers musicians and creators with complete career autonomy through AI-powered tools, financial services, healthcare benefits, and IP protection in one unified platform.

## Access

**Login:** Click "Get Started" on the homepage to create your account  
**Plans:** Free tier available, upgrade to Pro ($29/month) for full features

## Powered by Advanced Technology

Boptone is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure designed to scale with the creator economy:

**Frontend:** React with TypeScript for responsive, type-safe interfaces  
**Backend:** Node.js with real-time API communication  
**Database:** MySQL with optimized queries for instant data access  
**Authentication:** Secure OAuth with role-based permissions  
**AI Integration:** Advanced AI for career guidance and content generation  
**Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN for 99.9% uptime

This is production-ready technology built to support millions of creators worldwide.

## Using Your Website

### Getting Started

Click "Get Started" on the homepage and choose your plan. Free tier includes basic profile, revenue tracking up to one thousand dollars monthly, limited AI advisor access, and analytics. Pro tier unlocks unlimited features including direct-to-fan store, IP protection, healthcare, and tour management.

### Artist Dashboard

Your command center displays real-time metrics including total revenue across all income streams, total streams from platforms, follower count from social media, and new opportunities detected by AI. Click quick action buttons to upload music, add products, view analytics, or access IP protection.

### AI Career Advisor

Click "AI Advisor" in the dashboard header to access your personal consultant. Ask questions about release timing, social media growth, merchandise pricing, or career strategy. The AI provides personalized guidance based on music industry best practices and your specific data.

### Merchandise Store

Navigate to "Merchandise Store" from the dashboard. Click "Add Product" to create items. Fill in product name, description, price, and inventory count. Select type as Physical for merchandise and vinyl, Digital for downloads, or Experience for meet and greets. Click "Create Product" to list your item. Products appear in a grid with inventory tracking and sales statistics.

### Financial Dashboard

Access "Financial Dashboard" to view revenue breakdown by source including streaming, merchandise, shows, and licensing. Track active loans with repayment progress. Click "Apply for Micro-Loan" to access royalty-backed financing. Enter loan amount and repayment term, review AI-powered risk assessment, and submit application for instant processing.

### IP Protection

Monitor copyright infringement in "IP Protection." View detected unauthorized use of your content. Click "Send DMCA Takedown" to file automated takedown notices. Track status from Detected to DMCA Sent to Resolved. Mark false positives to improve AI accuracy.

### Tour Management

Plan live performances in "Tour Management." Click "Plan New Tour" to create a tour. Enter tour name, start and end dates, budget, and revenue projections. Add venue details for each show. Track actual revenue versus projections.

### Healthcare & Wellness

Enroll in artist-focused health coverage. Click "Enroll in Plan" to view options. Choose Basic at ninety-nine dollars monthly, Standard at one hundred ninety-nine dollars monthly, or Premium at three hundred forty-nine dollars monthly. Review benefits including mental health therapy, vocal specialist access, and twenty-four seven medical concierge. Click "Enroll Now" to activate coverage.

## Managing Your Website

### Settings Panel

Access Settings from the Management UI on the right side of the interface.

**General Settings:** Update website name and logo, configure visibility settings for public versus private access.

**Domains:** Modify auto-generated domain prefix or bind custom domains for professional branding.

**Secrets:** View and edit environment variables safely. Update API keys for external integrations like Spotify and Stripe.

### Dashboard Panel

Monitor platform performance with real-time visitor analytics, user engagement metrics, and system health monitoring.

### Database Panel

Manage data directly through the CRUD interface. View all database tables including artists, products, and tours. Edit records, add test data, or export for analysis. Full connection info available in bottom-left settings with SSL enabled for production.

### Publishing

When ready to launch, save a checkpoint in the Management UI, then click "Publish" button in the header. Your platform goes live on auto-scaling infrastructure with CDN.
