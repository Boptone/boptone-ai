**Your Mission Control for Artist Career Success**

## Purpose

Boptone empowers musicians and creators with end-to-end career management from discovery to breakthrough success. Access AI-powered tools, financial services, healthcare benefits, and IP protection all in one platform.

## Access

**Website:** Available after deployment via Manus platform  
**Login:** Click "Get Started" on the homepage to authenticate with Manus OAuth

## Powered by Manus

Boptone is built on cutting-edge technology that rivals AWS-level infrastructure:

**Frontend:** React 19 with TypeScript for type-safe, responsive interfaces  
**Backend:** Node.js with Express and tRPC for real-time API communication  
**Database:** MySQL/TiDB with Drizzle ORM for scalable data management

**Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN for 99.9% uptime

This is production-ready, enterprise-grade infrastructure designed to scale with the creator economy through 2030 and beyond.

## Using Your Website

### Getting Started

After logging in, you will be prompted to "Complete Your Profile." Click "Get Started" to set up your artist profile with your stage name, genre, and career phase.

### Artist Dashboard

Your central command center displays real-time metrics:
- **Total Revenue** across all income streams
- **Total Streams** from all platforms
- **Follower Count** aggregated from social media
- **New Opportunities** detected by AI

Click the quick action buttons to navigate to key features:
- "Upload Music" to distribute releases
- "Add Product" to manage merchandise
- "View Analytics" for detailed insights
- "IP Protection" to monitor copyright

### AI Career Advisor

Click "AI Advisor" in the dashboard header to access your personal AI consultant. Ask questions like:
- "Should I release my single now or wait for summer?"
- "How can I grow my Instagram following?"
- "What's the best pricing for my merch?"

The AI provides personalized guidance based on music industry best practices and your specific career data.

### Merchandise Store

Navigate to "Merchandise Store" from the dashboard to:
- Click "Add Product" to create new items (physical, digital, or experiences)
- Fill in product name, description, price, and inventory count
- Select product type (Physical for merch/vinyl, Digital for downloads, Experience for meet & greets)
- Click "Create Product" to list your item

Products appear in a grid with inventory tracking and sales stats.

### Financial Dashboard

Access "Financial Dashboard" to:
- View revenue breakdown by source (streaming, merch, shows, licensing)
- Track active loans with repayment progress
- Click "Apply for Micro-Loan" to access royalty-backed financing
  - Enter loan amount and repayment term
  - Review AI-powered risk assessment
  - Submit application for instant processing

### IP Protection

Monitor copyright infringement in "IP Protection":
- View detected unauthorized use of your content
- Click "Send DMCA Takedown" to file automated takedown notices
- Track status from "Detected" → "DMCA Sent" → "Resolved"
- Mark false positives to improve AI accuracy

### Tour Management

Plan live performances in "Tour Management":
- Click "Plan New Tour" to create a tour
- Enter tour name, start/end dates, budget, and revenue projections
- Add venue details for each show
- Track actual revenue vs. projections

### Healthcare & Wellness

Enroll in artist-focused health coverage:
- Click "Enroll in Plan" to view options
- Choose Basic ($99/month), Standard ($199/month), or Premium ($349/month)
- Review benefits including mental health therapy, vocal specialist access, and 24/7 medical concierge
- Click "Enroll Now" to activate coverage

## Managing Your Website

### Settings Panel

Access the Settings panel from the Management UI (right side of interface):

**General Settings:**
- Update website name and logo via VITE_APP_TITLE and VITE_APP_LOGO
- Configure visibility settings for public vs. private access

**Domains:**
- Modify auto-generated domain prefix (xxx.manus.space)
- Bind custom domains for professional branding

**Secrets:**
- View and edit environment variables safely
- Update API keys for external integrations (Spotify, Stripe, etc.)

### Dashboard Panel

Monitor platform performance:
- View real-time visitor analytics (UV/PV)
- Track user engagement metrics
- Review system health and uptime

### Database Panel

Manage data directly through CRUD interface:
- View all database tables (artists, products, tours, etc.)
- Edit records, add test data, or export for analysis
- Full connection info available in bottom-left settings (enable SSL for production)

### Publishing

When ready to launch:
1. Save a checkpoint via "Save Checkpoint" in the Management UI
2. Click "Publish" button in the header (top-right)
3. Your platform goes live on Manus infrastructure with auto-scaling and CDN
