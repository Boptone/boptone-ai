### 1. The Sovereign Artist Principle
The primary unit of value is the individual creator and their direct relationship with their audience. All system functions must enhance this sovereignty, not create new dependencies.

### 2. Protocols Over Platforms
Value and community longevity are built on open, verifiable systems (protocols), not on rented land controlled by third-party platforms. The OS should be a gateway to these protocols, not a walled garden.

### Module 1: Digital Identity & IP Registry
**Ingest:**
- WIPO copyright frameworks
- NFT metadata standards (ERC-721, ERC-1155, CIP-25)
- Verifiable Credentials (W3C)
- Getty Images IP taxonomy

**Function:** Create a non-refundable, sovereign digital identity for the artist that anchors all IP. Every song, stem, sample, and visual asset is minted as a verifiable, timestamped asset linked to this identity. This is the system's "source of truth."

### Module 2: Smart Contract Treasury & Royalty Router
**Ingest:**
- Ethereum Request for Comment (ERC) standards
- Royalty Registry APIs
- DAO treasury management models (Gnosis Safe)
- Blockchain gas fee mechanics
- Cross-chain bridging protocols (LayerZero, Wormhole)

**Function:** A vault that holds assets (tokens, NFTs) and automatically executes financial logic. It splits streaming micro-payments, distributes fan investment dividends, and pays collaborators based on pre-programmed, immutable agreements.

### Module 3: Community & Governance Engine
**Ingest:**
- DAO tooling (Snapshot for voting, Coordinape for rewards)
- Web3 social graphs (Lens Protocol, Farcaster)
- Tokenomics models (bonding curves, staking mechanics)
- CRM principles

**Function:** Manages token-gated access, facilitates community voting on creative/business decisions (e.g., "Which single should we promote?"), and distributes rewards to engaged community members.

### Module 4: AI Co-Creation & Management Layer
**Ingest:**
- Stable Diffusion & LLM fine-tuning processes
- Voice model training ethics (Holly Herndon's "Digital Twin")
- AI copyright case law (Getty Images v. Stability AI)
- Style transfer algorithms

**Function:** Allows artists to train their own ethical AI models on their owned IP (voice, production style). Manages the licensing of these models to third parties, with all usage tracked and royalties routed back to the treasury.

### Module 5: Cross-Metaverse Deployment & Licensing
**Ingest:**
- Open Metaverse Interoperability standards
- Unity/Unreal Engine audio integration
- Spatial audio formats
- Virtual land economics (Decentraland, The Sandbox parcels)

**Function:** Packages music and sonic assets for deployment across virtual worlds, gaming engines, and VR/AR experiences. Acts as a licensing portal for virtual world builders to easily license music for their spaces.
